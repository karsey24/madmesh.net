madmesh.net aims to be helpful for anyone curious about the Mesh, regardless of their skill level.

### What we're looking for

**More content:** build guides, CLI/configuration guides, and useful photos.
**New information:** mesh technology and best-practices are always on the move. Help us keep up with what's new.
**Documentation:** many Mesh projects are open-source and continue to develop based on the passion of their maintainers. Documentation is often lacking. Where possible, madmesh.net will attempt to offer documentation. Please be thorough: if we're doing documentation, let's make this website the only one that anyone has to visit for a particular topic or issue.
**Corrections:** typos and mistakes happen, so please remain vigilent and don't be afraid to submit a PR even if it's over a single letter!

### What we're not looking for

**Big changes:** when we want to redesign the website, we'll make a new project out of it.
**Overly-subjective or false content:** personal anecdotes, AI-hallucinated information, and conjecture are not desirable.
**Plagiarism and privacy:** do not submit code, images, or any other content which you do not have permission to publish.
**Needless changes:** commits with lots of refactoring, messed up line endings, or no net improvement of the website will be rejected.
